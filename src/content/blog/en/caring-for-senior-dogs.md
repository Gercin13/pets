Aging isn't a disease to fix — it's a stage to support, with a few thoughtful adjustments at home.

## Comfort starts with the floor

Slippery floors are harder on aging joints than most owners realize. A supportive orthopedic bed and non-slip rugs on hard floors do more for daily comfort than almost anything else.

## Signs worth a vet visit, not just "old age"

- Sudden reluctance to climb stairs or jump onto furniture
- Noticeably increased thirst or urination
- Cloudy eyes paired with bumping into furniture
- Any new lump, however small, that wasn't there before

> Slowing down is normal in a senior dog — sudden change is not, and it's always worth a second look.
