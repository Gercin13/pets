The first month sets the tone for your whole relationship — slow and predictable beats exciting and overwhelming.

## Start small, then expand

Confine your kitten to one quiet room at first, with food, water, a litter box, and a hiding spot, all placed well apart. Let it explore the rest of the home gradually, once it seeks it out on its own.

## A rhythm kittens settle into fast

- Keep feeding times consistent — kittens thrive on routine
- Schedule the first vet visit within the first week
- Introduce other pets slowly, through a closed door at first
- Expect a few nights of vocalizing — it usually fades within a week

> A kitten that hides for the first few days isn't rejecting you — it's just deciding the room is safe enough to come out.
