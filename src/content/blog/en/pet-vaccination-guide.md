Vaccination is the single most effective thing you can do to protect your pet from preventable, often fatal disease.

## Why the timing matters

A puppy or kitten's early immunity comes from its mother's milk — protection that fades over the first few months. Vaccines bridge that gap, so giving them **too early or too late** leaves a dangerous window open.

## Core vs. non-core vaccines

- **Core** — recommended for every pet
- **Non-core** — based on lifestyle and region

> A vaccinated pet isn't just protected — it protects every animal it meets.
