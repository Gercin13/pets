A calm road trip starts well before the car does — most travel stress comes from what wasn't prepared in advance.

## Get them used to the car first

If your pet only rides in the car to the vet, it has learned to dread it. Take a few short, fun drives beforehand so the car itself stops being the stressful part.

## What to pack, beyond the obvious

- Water, a familiar bowl, and food for the entire trip plus a buffer day
- A copy of vaccination records, especially for crossing state or country lines
- A secured carrier or harness — never loose in the car
- A recent photo, in case of an unexpected escape at a rest stop

> The goal isn't a perfect trip — it's one where your pet still trusts the car afterward.
