The cutest puppy in the litter isn't always the right fit — a breed's energy and needs matter far more than its looks.

## Start with your daily reality

Be honest about your schedule, living space, and activity level. A **high-energy working breed** left alone in a small apartment all day will struggle, no matter how much you love it.

## Four questions worth answering first

- How many hours of exercise can you realistically give, every day?
- Do you have the time (and patience) for a heavy-shedding, high-grooming coat?
- Is anyone in the household allergic, or nervous around large dogs?
- Are you prepared for a breed prone to specific health conditions?

> The best breed for you isn't the most popular one — it's the one whose needs match the life you actually live.
