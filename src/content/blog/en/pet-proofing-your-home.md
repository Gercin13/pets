Most household accidents happen in the rooms owners consider the safest.

## Kitchen and bathroom first

Trash cans, dangling cords, and cabinet cleaners cause more emergency visits than almost anything else. A simple **child-lock latch** solves most of it in minutes.

## A quick room-by-room pass

- Kitchen: secure the trash, store chocolate and xylitol out of reach
- Living room: tuck away cords, tie up blind cords, check for small swallowable parts
- Bathroom: close the toilet lid, store medication in a latched cabinet
- Garage: lock away antifreeze — it's sweet-tasting and highly toxic

> A pet-proofed home isn't sterile — it's just one that's been looked at from paw height.
